ROUGE ABSOLU at Bergdorf Goodman in New York

New York City News
21 Jun 2021, 10 GMT+10

A New York landmark since 1901, the very prestigious and luxurious Bergdorf Goodman fashion and lifestyle store located at the crossroads of 5th Avenue and 58th Street, has been presenting pieces signed ROUGE ABSOLU by Geraldine Prieur for several years.

Commissioned to bring the colorful French Touch, the ROUGE ABSOLU studio sets the tone in the private rooms reserved for VIPs. These are stagings magnified by the magic of Geraldine Prieur and her creation studio that stun the beautiful brands of tableware and home accessories from the prestigious store.

The ROUGE ABSOLU creative studio provides a unique journey and experience in terms of Interior Design, Scenography, Design, and Fashion.

The ROUGE ABSOLU Galerie located in the heart of the famous Carre Rive Gauche in Saint-Germain-des-Pres, Paris, encourages a luxurious take away.

Geraldine PRIEUR defines a new esthetic imposes design luxury.

The luxuriance of her worlds attracts luxury hotels, retail and residential projects around the world.

Graldine Prieur dares with visionary Design and a sensual signature.

ROUGE (RED), a symbol of life, of luxury, of sensuality, of desire...

ABSOLU (ABSOLUTE), expresses radicalism in its esthetic choices as well as its commitments.
